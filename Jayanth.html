  2. Project-2: LIGHT FOLLOWING ROBOT


    Team size: 3
    Prerequisites: 2x LDR,2x BC547 Transistors,2x 2 x 30 ohms Resistors, 2 x Gear motor wheels,1 x multi-directional wheel, 9-volt Battery.
    Project description: The light sensing robot moves to the direction of light. If the light falls on the left side, will move towards the left side and same for right side. If the light on both sides is same then it will go straight. The model contains sensors (LDR), comparators, drivers, motors, light sources and power source. Normally LDR has very high dark resistance, but when it is illuminated with light, resistance drops. The voltage across this resistor is fed to the comparator, which compares this voltage with a reference voltage and gives output according to that.
